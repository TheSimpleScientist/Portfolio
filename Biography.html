The Clifton StrengthsFinder is a widely-known online assessment of a person's natural areas of strength, based on the theories of late psychologist Don Clifton.

There are 34 themes of strength that StrengthsFinder assesses a person on, and the 5 themes that they score the highest in are that person's "signature themes".
